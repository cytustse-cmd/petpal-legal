常见问题 / FAQ

  • 如何删除我的账号？ 打开 App → 设置 → 账户 → 删除账户。该操作会清除你存储在 iCloud 上的所有数据，且不可恢复。
  • How do I delete my account? Open the app → Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your iCloud-synced data.
  • 订阅如何取消？ 在 iPhone 「设置 → Apple ID → 订阅」中管理 PetPal Pro 订阅。
  • How do I cancel my subscription? Manage your PetPal Pro subscription in iOS Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • 家庭成员如何加入？ Pro 用户可在「家庭」页生成邀请链接（petpal://invite?code=...），成员点击即可加入。
  • How do family members join? Pro users can generate an invite link in the Family tab; members tap the link to join.
